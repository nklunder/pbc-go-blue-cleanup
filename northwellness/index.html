    LifeWise Health Plans are designed to help Washingtonians enjoy Northwellness. We have simple, easy-to-use plans at a market-leading low price. Most of our plans include:

    • An extensive regional network with access to doctors and hospitals across the Pacific Northwest, including most major health systems in Washington
    • Two no-cost primary care doctor visits
    • Unlimited online or phone access to a board-certified physician for just $15 per visit
    • Coverage for preventive checkups and most vaccinations, including flu shots
    • Thousands of exclusive discounts on your favorite health and lifestyle activities
